Privacy Policy for Grid School

1. Introduction
Grid School ("we," "us," or "our") uses LinkedIn’s APIs to provide functionalities such as posting on your behalf. This policy explains how we handle personal data obtained through LinkedIn integrations.

2. Data Collection
When you connect your LinkedIn account with our service, we receive basic profile data (e.g., name, profile ID) and any additional data you explicitly grant access to. We do not collect other personal information unless you provide it voluntarily.

3. Data Usage
We use your data solely to enable posting to LinkedIn and related functionalities within our application. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

4. Data Retention
We store the minimum amount of data required to operate the service. Any stored data is kept only as long as necessary for our stated purposes or as required by law.

5. User Rights
You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. If you revoke our app’s permissions on LinkedIn, we will no longer have access to your LinkedIn data.

6. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the data we store from unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at inquire@gridschool.org
